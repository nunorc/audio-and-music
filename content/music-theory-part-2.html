Triplets

  • Group of 3 equal beats, to be played in the time of 2 beats
  • Described by a bracket (or beam) with a number 3

Minor Scales

  • Three flavours of minor scales
  • Natural (or relative) minor: follow the relative key signature
  • Harmonic minor: 6th degree of the natural minor is raised
  • Melodic minor: different ascending descending

Harmonic Minor Scales

  • A scale is any predefined series of notes
  • The distance between notes defines the tonality of the scale
  • The distance can be a whole tone (T) or a semitone (S)
  • Harmonic minor scales pattern is T-S-T-T-S-3S-S

Melodic Minor Scales

  • Use the harmonic minor scale as a starting point
  • Ascending: raise the 6th degree by a semitone
  • Descending: lower the 7th degree by a semitone
