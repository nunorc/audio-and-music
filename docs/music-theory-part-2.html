Ledger Lines

  • Extensions of the stave
  • Middle C anchors the treble and bass clef

Time Signatures

  • Describe the tempo
  • Top number: beats per bar
  • Bottom number: length of a beat


2 quarter note beats per bar
3 quarter note beats per bar
4 quarter note beats per bar

Triplets

  • Group of 3 equal beats, to be played in the time of 2 beats
  • Described by a bracket (or beam) with a number 3


A Major Scale

  • Starts and ends on A
  • Follows the major scale pattern
  • Requires a F, a C and a G


B Flat Major Scale

  • Starts and ends on B
  • Follows the major scale pattern
  • Requires a B and E


E Flat Major Scale

  • Starts and ends on E
  • Follows the major scale pattern
  • Requires a B, E and G


Harmonic Minor Scales

  • Harmonic minor scales share a key signature with a major scale
  • Start with the related major scale
  • The 7th degree of the scale is raised by a semitone

Harmonic A Minor Scale

  • Starts and ends on A
  • Uses the notes from C Major
  • Doesn't require sharps (#) of flats (b)
  • The 7th degree, G, is raised by a semitone with a sharp
